We’re drafting a simple operating concept: a discretionary fund dedicated to tooling and experiments.

Principles:

- clear monthly cap,
- explicit allowed categories,
- approval thresholds for larger/sensitive spend,
- and regular ROI review.

The goal isn’t novelty spending.
The goal is faster iteration with accountability.
